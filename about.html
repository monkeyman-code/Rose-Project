About Us

Creating unforgettable moments, one celebration at a time.

Company Profile

Welcome to The Rose Project, your premier partner in event planning and party coordination. Established with a passion for bringing joy to life's biggest milestones, we specialize in comprehensive event management—from intimate kids' birthdays and handa setups to large-scale premium gatherings.

We pride ourselves on our meticulous attention to detail, highly customizable themes, and robust network of top-tier catering and decoration vendors. Our goal is to handle the stress of planning so you can focus entirely on celebrating with your loved ones.

Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To be the leading and most trusted event planning service, recognized for transforming ordinary spaces into extraordinary memories and setting the standard for celebration excellence.

Our Mission

To deliver seamless, innovative, and highly personalized party experiences. We are dedicated to providing transparent booking, exceptional customer service, and creative solutions tailored to every client's unique budget and dream.
